Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) sits at the heart of modern genomics and transcriptomics. It powers everything from personalized medicine to our understanding of evolution. If you are beginning your journey in bioinformatics, understanding how sequencing works is not just helpful, it is essential. This course is designed to walk you through where biological data actually comes from, how it is generated in the lab, and what it truly represents. By the end, you will have the clarity and confidence needed to interpret sequencing data and fully appreciate the powerful tools and technologies you will encounter as you move forward.
